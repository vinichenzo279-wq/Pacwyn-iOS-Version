The highest team rating the solver assumes is reachable — it's a speed lever. Real drafts rarely break the mid-90s, so a lower ceiling searches far fewer nodes. Lower it if solving feels slow; raise it late in the season when higher-rated cards appear. 96 is the recommended default — raise it if you believe your draft can be rated higher.
